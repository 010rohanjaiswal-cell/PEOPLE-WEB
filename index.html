Why we built People

Clients struggle

Finding the right person for a task is frustrating.

  • You don’t know who is genuine
  • Prices are unclear
  • Communication is messy
  • Time gets wasted in follow-ups & bargaining

Skilled workers struggle

Talent exists—but opportunities don’t reach everyone.

  • No stable leads
  • Reliance on word-of-mouth
  • Low visibility
  • Lack of trust blocks work

One platform solves both

People creates a trusted local work ecosystem.

  • Structured job posting
  • Applications & offers (no calling needed)
  • Clear status tracking
  • Verified profiles to build trust

How It Works

Client side (Hiring made easy)

Post a job in seconds, set your budget, and choose the best match.

  • Post from 12 categories (and searchable “Other”)
  • Add details, budget, and location
  • Track status in My Jobs + History
  • Accept applications or offers—no bargaining calls

Freelancer side (Earning made simple)

Find nearby work, apply directly, or send offers—then track assigned jobs.

  • Available Jobs with category filtering (multi-select)
  • Apply or send offers
  • My Jobs shows assigned work + status
  • Silent refresh + smart guidance when assigned

Smart accessibility

Hindi translation + caching make the app usable for more people.

  • Hindi translation for job details & subcategories
  • Translations cached for faster next sessions
  • Location-based relevance
  • Support chat inside the app

Key Features

Category-based posting

Jobs start with categories. “Other” includes searchable subcategories for precision.

Hindi translation + caching

Translations are cached so users don’t wait again in new sessions.

Location-based matching

Nearby jobs show up first. The app guides users if GPS is off.

Offers & applications

Freelancers apply or send offers. Clients choose the best match.

In-app support chat

Support page + guided quick options, like a proper ticket flow inside the app.

Faster loading with caching

Screens show previous data instantly and refresh in the background.
