About People App

People connects clients who need work done with freelancers who have real skills. Our moto is simple: “Any job. Any skill. One platform.”

We’re building a trusted local work ecosystem where hiring becomes easy and earning opportunities become accessible—especially for skilled people without degrees or formal job access.
